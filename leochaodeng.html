Leo Deng is a Chinese artist currently living and working in Australia. His practice explores the tension between structure, collapse, and control through woodworking, digital fabrication, lo-fi construction, and material experimentation.

Influenced by artists from socialist and post-socialist contexts, Leo’s work reflects how built forms embody history, power, and ideology. His cross-cultural background further informs his approach to form, labor, and technology.
